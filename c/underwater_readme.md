Key Features of the Underwater Exploration Simulator:

1. Unique Theme: Explore a vibrant underwater world instead of a city
2. Depth Mechanics: Different depth levels affect oxygen consumption
3. Water Currents: Dynamic water currents that affect movement
4. Oxygen System: Manage your oxygen levels as you explore deeper
5. Treasure Hunting: Find hidden treasures for points
6. Marine Life: Various sea creatures with different behaviors
7. Vertical Movement: Use space to ascend and 'C' to descend
8. Scoring System: Earn points by discovering treasures

How to Play:

· Use WASD to move horizontally
· Press Space to ascend (move up)
· Press C to descend (move down)
· Find treasures to increase your score
· Avoid sharks as they deplete your oxygen
· Manage your oxygen level - it decreases faster at greater depths
